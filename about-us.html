About Us

CapVisor Associates, LLC

Founded in 2008 as an SEC-registered investment advisory firm focused exclusively on serving the insurance marketplace.

Our Mission

CapVisor’s mission is to improve investment program results with solutions uniquely crafted to address the needs of small to midsize commercial and alternative risk insurers. Many experts recognize that insurers in these market segments are often overcharged and underserved by consultants/advisors or their investment managers under the traditional business models.

Most investment service providers are “generalists” or specialized in serving other institutional investors (pensions, 401(k)s, endowments and foundations, public funds, etc.) or are focused on the high net worth market (wealth management). Their expertise, analytic systems, portfolio construction process, investment objectives, and fee structures are typically “one size fits all” and often inadequate or inappropriate for insurance companies.

We recognize Insurance Asset Management as a discrete discipline requiring expertise and analytics specifically designed to address the challenges inherent in managing the assets of insurance companies. It requires an extensive understanding of insurance underwriting activities and the interrelationship between the liability structure and the investment plan. CapVisor only works with insurance company clients, optimizing their investment results by correlating their investment strategy with their company’s business objectives, risk tolerance, and other constraints.

Our Team

Trusted, seasoned insurance asset management professionals

Carl Terzer

Principal and Founder

Carl Terzer is Founder and Principal of CapVisor Associates, LLC. He brings more than 37 years of insurance asset management experience to the task of correlating clients’ investment strategy with their business objectives, risk tolerance, liability structure, and accounting and regulatory environments. His expertise encompasses investment plan design, strategic asset allocation, tactical portfolio optimization, manager search/evaluation, and after-tax, risk-adjusted performance analysis. Mr. Terzer’s experience spans insurers of all sizes and lifecycle stages: from 831(b)s to multi-billion-dollar internationals, and from start-ups to run-offs.

Mr. Terzer is currently active in NAMIC, PCIA, ACLI, and numerous alternative risk market organizations. Along with AM Best Company, he has designed and implemented an investment educational program consisting of CapVisor training classes for AM Best analysts seeking CPE credits. He is an Instructor for NAMIC’s Mutual Director Certification (MDC) Program (CapVisor is also a program sponsor), and the Investment Instructor and curriculum designer at the International Center for Captive Insurance Education (ICCIE), helping students achieve their Associate in Captive Insurance (ACI) designation. Mr. Terzer serves or has served on various Boards and committees of commercial insurers as well as captive industry organizations.

Mr. Terzer was formerly the Director of the Insurance Advisory Group of Principal Global Investors. Previously, he was Senior Vice President and Marketing Director of the Insurance Asset Management Group of Brown Brothers Harriman & Co. His experience also includes various senior executive positions at Bankers Trust Company, Chase Manhattan Bank, and MetLife.

As a frequent speaker at insurance-related events, Mr. Terzer is also regularly sought out to provide editorial contributions to leading insurance industry publications such as Reactions, Insurance Asset Manager, US Insurer, Rough Notes, The Self-Insurer, Captive.com, and CICR. Carl received a Bachelor’s Degree in Business Administration and Economics from Rutgers University.

Debbie Leich

Managing Director

Debbie Leich is a Managing Director responsible for client relationship management and business development. SVP & Regional Director
