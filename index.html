The CapVisor Difference

Is your allocation optimized for your business?

For insurers, asset allocation is the single most important driver of long-term investment performance. Across decades of institutional studies, strategic asset allocation explains roughly 70–90% of long-term portfolio returns, while active management — security selection, market timing, tactical tilts — accounts for only about 10–30%, often less after fees.

Our allocation process is rooted in a deep understanding of insurance company investment constraints: reserve requirements, surplus structure, regulatory treatment, and duration sensitivity. Using proprietary tools and institutional capital market assumptions, we construct customized allocation frameworks that reflect your unique profile, risk appetite, and financial goals.

We don’t sell models, we build them around you.

CapVisor works exclusively with insurance companies. That specialization gives us a critical advantage: we understand the regulatory, operational, and capital implications of every investment decision you make — whether we’re adjusting surplus allocations, reviewing fixed income strategies, or evaluating alternatives.
