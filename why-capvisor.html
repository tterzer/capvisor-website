The CapVisor Difference

Data-Driven. Insurance-Focused. Results-Oriented.

If your current investment “advisor” isn’t talking to you about asset allocation, risk-adjusted efficiency, tax efficiency, surplus optimization, RBC impact, and manager performance ranking, then they’re not seeing the full picture.

At CapVisor Associates, we believe that investment success isn’t achieved through guesswork, storytelling, or brand familiarity — it’s achieved through discipline, objectivity, and data. That is the CapVisor difference.

Unlike many advisors who rely on brand recognition or industry reputation, we rely on quantitative evidence. Our investment manager due diligence process begins with a robust analytical screen that evaluates performance consistency, downside protection, capital efficiency, risk-adjusted returns, and fee alignment. Every recommendation we make is supported by data and tested against your specific needs.

Qualitative considerations — team structure, philosophy, process — do play a role. However, in our disciplined investment decision-making framework, they are secondary and tertiary, not primary. We believe decisions should be quantitatively and qualitatively substantiated before they are finalized in the boardroom.

CapVisor works exclusively with insurance companies. That specialization gives us a critical advantage: we understand the regulatory, operational, and capital implications of every investment decision you make. Whether we’re adjusting surplus allocations, reviewing fixed income strategies, or evaluating alternatives, we’re always asking:

  • How will this impact your RBC ratio?
  • Will this strategy reduce risk or introduce excess volatility?
  • Does this move support your long-term business objectives?

We measure success not just by performance, but by program integrity. That means your portfolio is aligned with liabilities, designed to withstand market shocks, and positioned to support financial stability across cycles.

Importantly, CapVisor is fully independent. We are non-discretionary and don’t manage investment products or strategies. We don’t have a stake in any strategy or group of managers that we may recommend for client consideration. Our only incentive is to serve your best interest with transparency, accountability, and precision.

  • Quantitative/qualitative manager selection following the CFA Institute’s prescribed process
  • Insurance-focused insights
  • Independent, unbiased advice that avoids the conflict of giving advice and then implementing that advice through proprietary products

Why insurers choose CapVisor

1. Dedicated

To the insurance industry and to insurance asset management. CapVisor exclusively services insurance companies, with a laser focus on small- to mid-sized insurers. Commercial insurers, mutuals, captives, RRGs, and other ART insurers are our specialty.

2. Experienced

150+ years of combined insurance asset management experience on our team, including over 35 years working with non-internally managed insurance portfolios — prior big-organization experience now offered through a boutique firm.

3. Expert

Our professionals have worked with insurers domestic and international, on- and off-shore, from small/start-up accounts to mature multi-billion-dollar international accounts and run-offs, investing across US and global fixed income, equity, and alternative securities.

4. Committed

We demonstrate investment leadership within the insurance industry as conference speakers and editorial contributors, members of major domicile associations, and recognized educational authorities — including as investment instructors for NAMIC’s Directors’ Boot Camp, ICCIE, and AM Best’s CPE-accredited internal training program.

5. Independent

We are an employee-owned insurance asset management consulting firm providing fair and balanced investment advice. We advise only and do not manage money, avoiding conflicts of interest — our compensation is never determined by or tied to a client’s investment choices.

6. Unique

A one-of-a-kind business model: Consulting/Outsourced CIO services, Manager Search services, the Steward Model Portfolio Series, and Project Work — kept structurally separate to avoid conflicts of interest.

7. Quantitative

Analysis and evaluation capabilities backed by industry-leading systems specifically designed to incorporate ALM for insurance companies, including manager universe and risk-adjusted return analysis and actuarially designed strategic asset allocation optimization.

8. Qualitative

Extensive knowledge of the insurance asset manager community versus the broader investment management community, insurance company lines of business and operations, and the network of intermediaries — actuaries, banks/trusts/LOC providers, accountants, attorneys — that surround a client’s investment program.

9. Cost Effective

Competitive, portfolio-market-value-based consulting fees; a no-fee Manager Search Service; and negotiated flat fees for project work.

10. Solution Based

Plan design (risk tolerance assessment, IPS, guidelines, benchmark review/design), strategic asset allocation advice or optimization, and on-going management/oversight of the investment plan.
